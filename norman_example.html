Applying Norman's 7 Stages – Splitwise "Who Paid?" Example

Nida paid the dinner bill. Using Splitwise, the expense must be equally divided among Ali, Bisma, Neha, and Sania.

Norman Stage Action / Explanation
Goal Distribute the dinner bill fairly among all friends.
Plan / Intention Use the Splitwise app to create a group and log the payment for proper splitting.
Specify Action Open Splitwise app → select Ali, Bisma, Neha, Sania → enter bill amounts 5-4-8-0 → confirm amounts → click Save.
Execute Perform all actions: opening app, selecting members, entering amounts, confirming, and saving.
Perceive Observe confirmation visuals: green checkmark, "Expense Added" message, updated balances displayed.
Interpret The expense is added and now each friend owes 1370 PKR. Notifications were sent to all.
Evaluate Verify notifications sent, balances updated, split calculated correctly, goal achieved.
